About Me

Who Am I?

Hi! I'm Mari Aoki. I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science in May 2022 and am now working towards a Master's in Computer Science at the University of New Mexico. I am interested in using machine learning technologies to aid in space exploration and hope to one day apply these methods to benefit humanity.

I believe technology is a major human asset that carries great potential in making our lives easier and solving issues that were once seen as irresolvable. I knew I wanted to focus on STEM since I was a little girl but had a difficult time choosing a major when coming to college. I realized that my knack for computers, aptitude to see patterns, and love of puzzles were a perfect fit for studying computer science. Even though my skills are in computer science, I have an unwavering fascination with all things space. From planetarium visits at my local museum to astronomy units in science classes to watching space movies, my interest in space has been around for a long time.

My unique background of having been a full-time undergraduate student while training and competing as a Division 1 student-athlete and being involved in extracurricular activities has given me valuable experiences. It made me someone who is always seeking new opportunities and people, as well as someone who is able to manage priorities. Outside of work you might find me trying a new recipe, looking up new workouts, or reading about various ways to better manage my life and balance myself. I have always been surrounded by a great community and am always looking for ways to connect with others.

This site is an ongoing project, thank you for your patience.
